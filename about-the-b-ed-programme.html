About the B.ED Programme

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) Programme is a two year professional programme in the field of teacher education which aims at preparing school teachers, for upper primary, secondary level (classes VI -X) and higher secondary level (PUC). Also aims at preparing, practitioners and other educational professionals including manpower for curriculum development, planners, administrators, supervisors, school principals and for extension activities
